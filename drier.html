Vacuum Drier-Rotar

Vacuum Driers are designed at a rotating speed of 12 rpm to 45 rpm for an operating condition up to -720 mm wc at temperature ranging from 130 to 170 degree C. It is a jacketed vessel with internal veins for hot water/ steam flow

Tray Drier

Tray dryers, from 300 kg per batch to 2990 kg per batch, have been designed and manufactured using food grade stainless steel material, for various pharmaceutical, chemical and process industries.

Tray driers are used to reduce the moisture content of intermediates and finished active ingredients by the circulation of large volume of filtered hot air which is generated using steam
