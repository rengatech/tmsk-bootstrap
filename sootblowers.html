SOOT BLOWERS

We have successfully designed, manufactured and supplied more than 550 soot blowers to our national and international clients. In an Industrial boiler, soot/ ash/ slag accumulates on the surface of coils/ tubes results in reduction of heat transfer. Soot deposition can also erode and corrode metal surface. It is necessary to remove this accumulation periodically in order to increase efficiency of boiler performance.

  • Long Retractable Soot Blowers
  • Rotary Motarised Soot Blowers
  • Wall Deslaggers / Short Retractable Soot Blowers
  • Rotary Manual Soot Blowers
Rotary Motorised Soot Blower

This is designed to remove soot/ ash/ slag deposited on tubes in economizer as well as boiler tubes in low temperature zone (below 750 deg C). The lance element remains inside boiler/ economiser for all the time.

Wall Deslagger
(Short Retractable Soot Blower)

We have successfully designed, manufactured and supplied more than 550 soot blowers to our national and international clients. In an Industrial boiler, soot/ ash/ slag accumulates on the surface of coils/ tubes results in reduction of heat transfer. Soot deposition can also erode and corrode metal surface. It is necessary to remove this accumulation periodically in order to increase efficiency of boiler performance.

Long Retractable Soot Blowers

Long Retractable Soot Blowers (LRSB) function is to remove soot deposited on coils / tubes in high temperature zone. To avoid sagging of Lance element from high temperature of flue gases, LRSB are parked outside the boiler. Ventury nozzles fitted on lancing element achieves high cleaning efficiency.
